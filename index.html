I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology (Excellence Class) at Fudan University . I am currently a senior and expect to receive the Bachelor of Engineering degree in June 2023. I'm now working as a research assistant at HKUST CSE department with Prof. Dan Xu, and also in the Mechanical Systems Control Lab , UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Wei Zhan. Previously, I have been working with Prof. Tao Chen and Prof. Jiayuan Fan at Fudan University.

My research interests lie in computer vision, multi-task learning and scene understanding. In particular, I want to find unified and effective paradigms for representation learning and knowledege transferring between various computer vision tasks, including the interactions and mutual learning involved in 3D perception and reconstruction, multi-modality perception, etc.
